In a weighted directed graph $G$ with a designated source node $s$ and sink node $t$, define:

* (F(s,t)): the largest achievable transfer quantity from (s) to (t) when respecting the capacities of the connections in the network; and
* (C(s)), (C(t)): values associated with (s) and (t) that reflect how densely their immediate neighboring nodes are interconnected when edge directions are ignored.

The **endpoint-aware flow score** is given by
$$
S = F(s,t) \cdot \Bigl(1 + \frac{C(s) + C(t)}{2}\Bigr).
$$

**Task.**
Given $G$, $s$, and $t$, evaluate this endpoint-aware flow score $S$ and return its value.
